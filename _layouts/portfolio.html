Hi! My name is Christina, and I have a passion for creating fun, beautiful, honest, and consistent experiences that span entire product systems.

I am a web designer and illustrator with a multi-disciplinary background in both studio art and interactive media. My work explores the intersection of fine art values, craftsmanship, and technology. I try to approach each project holistically, with an open mind and a wide breadth of experience. I spend most of my waking life between the Creative Suite and Sublime Text, but I am not afraid to take projects off the screen and into the real world with custom multimedia solutions.
