Hi! My name is Christina, and I have a passion for creating fun, beautiful, honest, and consistent experiences that span entire product systems.

With a multi-disciplinary background in both studio art and interactive media, my work is drawn from the intersection of fine art values, craftsmanship, and technology. I approach each project with an open mind and a wide breadth of experience. I spend most of my waking life between the Creative Suite and Sublime Text, but I am not afraid to take projects off the screen and into the real world with custom multimedia solutions.

I draw a lot of inspiration from my hobbies. When I’m not pushing pixels or Git commits, you can find me hanging out with a corgi named Penny, planting flowers, seasoning cast iron pans, reading mid-century Japanese literature, tinkering with my Arduino, or playing my clarinet. Ideally all at the same time.
